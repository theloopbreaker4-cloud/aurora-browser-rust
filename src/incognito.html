About Incognito

Incognito mode opens a separate window with an ephemeral profile. When you close the window, all browsing data from that session is discarded.

  • Browsing history is not saved
  • Cookies are isolated and discarded on close
  • Form autofill data is not saved
  • Downloads are kept (you can delete them manually)

What Incognito does NOT hide

  • Your activity from the websites you visit
  • Your activity from your employer, school, or ISP
  • Your IP address from the sites you connect to
